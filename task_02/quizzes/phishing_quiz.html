🧠 Phishing Awareness Quiz

Q1: You receive an email from your bank asking you to verify your login. What should you do?

  • Click the link and enter credentials
  • Ignore and delete it
  • Contact the bank via their official number ✅

Q2: The URL says "micr0soft-support.com" — is it safe?

  • Yes, it has 'Microsoft' in the name
  • No, it's a typo-squatted phishing site ✅
